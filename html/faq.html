Frequently Asked Questions

For Towride users

1. What is Towride?

Towride is a combinative commune assistance service that provides towing and riding services to drivers in need, especially to drunk drivers.

2. How does Towride work?

Users can request assistance through the Towride mobile application, website, and/or hotline. Once a request is received, a nearby towride vehicle is dispatched to the user's location to tow the vehicle and ride the drivers (and passengers if any) to the destination(s).

3. What types of services does Towride offer?

Towride offers only two services, towing and riding.

4. Is Towride available 24/7?

Yes. Towride operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing round-the-clock assistance to drivers in need of combinative towing and riding services.

5. How long does it take for assistance to arrive?

Response times may vary depending on factors such as distance, location, traffic conditions, and demand levels. However, Towride strives to dispatch assistance promptly to ensure timely service.

6. Can I track the status of my service request?

Yes. Users can track the status of their service request through the Towride mobile application or website, providing real-time updates on the location and estimated time of arrival of the dispatched assistance vehicle.

7. How much does Towride cost?

The total cost of Towride services may vary depending on the distance towed and ridden. The basic rate is $30 covering 5 miles, and the extra mileage price is $3/mile. Users are informed of the applicable fees before confirming their Towride service request.

8. Is Towride available nationwide?

Towride services may be available in certain regions or states, and coverage areas may vary. Users are encouraged to check the availability of Towride services in their area through the mobile application or website.

9. How do I pay for Towride services?

Payment for Towride services is processed electronically through secure payment methods accepted by the platform, such as credit/debit cards or digital wallets.

10. Can I cancel my Towride service request?

Yes. Users can cancel their service request through the Towride mobile application or by contacting customer support. However, cancellation fees may apply depending on the timing of the cancellation.



For Hotshot users

1. What is Hotshot?

Hotshot is a local online and offline express delivery system, a service that facilitates the quick and efficient transportation of goods from one location to another within a local area.

2. How does Hotshot work?

Users can request our Hotshot services through our website, mobile application, and hotline. Once a request is placed, a delivery driver is dispatched to pick up the package and transport it to the designated destination.

3. What types of items can be delivered?

Hotshot can transport a variety of items, including documents, parcels, groceries, food orders, and other small to medium-sized goods.

4. Is Hotshot available 24/7?

Yes. We offer round-the-clock service.

5. How long does it take for a delivery to arrive?

Delivery times can vary depending on factors such as distance, traffic conditions, and the urgency of the delivery. Generally, Hotshot aims to complete deliveries within one hour of the request.

6. Can I track the status of my delivery?

Yes. We offer tracking capabilities through our website or mobile application, allowing users to monitor the progress of their delivery in real-time.

7. Are there size or weight limitations for items that can be delivered?

Yes. Hotshot imposes size and weight restrictions on items that can be transported. Users are advised to check the specific limitations before requesting a delivery.

8. How much does Hotshot cost?

The cost of Hotshot may vary depending on factors such as distance, delivery urgency, and the size/weight of the package. Users are typically informed of the delivery fee before confirming their request.

9. What happens if my delivery is delayed or lost?

In the rare event of a delay or loss, users can contact customer support for assistance. Hotshot may offer compensation or alternative solutions depending on the circumstances.

10. Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

Hotshot allows users to schedule deliveries for a future date and time, providing flexibility and convenience for users with specific delivery requirements. If you have any further questions or concerns about Hotshot, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team for assistance.
